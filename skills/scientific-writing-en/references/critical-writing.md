- This argument is inconsistent regarding…
- Author's argument leans too heavily on…
- Author's understanding of X put forward in Y is questionable since…
- Author's interpretation dominates much of the scientific literature in our field; however,…
- A number of authors have discussed X on the grounds that…
- The analysis of X does not take into account…
- Another problem with this approach is that it fails to take into account…
- A clear disadvantage of this method is…
- Difficulties arise when…
- A major disadvantage of this approach is…

- Approaches of this kind carry serious limitations regarding…
- All the studies reviewed to date are limited in…
- These explanations tend to overlook the fact that…
- One of the problems with X is…
- The study by Author is regarded as the most relevant research to date on…; however, it is limited in…
