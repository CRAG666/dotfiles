## Time phrases by tense

Time markers conventionally pair with a specific tense in academic prose; mismatches read as errors to reviewers.

### Simple past - specific times or periods

- In year X,...
- From year X to year Y,...
- During the X period,...
- For centuries,...
- Throughout the Xth century,...
- At the start of the Xth century,...
- In the latter / second half of the Xth century,...
- Toward the end of the Xth century,...
- In the early years of the Xth century,...
- Half a century later,...
- Following X (event),...

### Past tense - describing research history

- The link between X and Y was established in year Z by Author.
- The association between X and Y was not demonstrated until year Z.
- Prior to the work of Author, the role of X was largely unknown.
- Before year X, Y had received only cursory attention.
- X was first articulated by Author and popularized in...
- It was not until the late X0s that researchers considered Y worthy of attention.
- Awareness of X is not recent, having possibly first been described in...

### Present perfect - past and present connected

- To date, little evidence has been found associating X with Y.
- Up to now, the research has tended to focus on X rather than Y.
- It is only since the work of Author that the study of X has gained momentum.
- So far, X factors have been identified as potentially important:...
- Since year X, Y has doubled...
- Until recently, there has been little interest in X.
- Only in the past X years have studies of Y directly addressed how...
- More recently, literature has emerged that offers contradictory findings about...
- Over the past century / decade / X years, there has been a dramatic increase in...
- The past decade has seen the rapid development of X.
- Over the past X decades, major advances in Y have allowed...

### Present perfect - recent research with several contributors

- Several studies have revealed that...
- Previous studies of X have not dealt with...
- X has been intensively investigated recently due to its...
- A considerable amount of literature has been published on X.
- X has been identified as a major contributing factor for...
- Factors thought to be influencing X have been explored in several studies.
- The relationship between X and Y has been widely investigated.
- There have been several investigations into the causes of X.

### Simple past - single investigations or publications

- The first systematic study of X was reported by Author in year Z.
- The first experimental realization of X, by Author, used a...
- An experimental demonstration of this effect was first carried out by...
- Author was the first to describe X, and reported that...
- X was originally isolated from Y in...
- X years later, Author reported...
- In year X, Author introduced a system of classification based on...
- In year X, Author published a paper in which they described...
- In year X, Author demonstrated that...
